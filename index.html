About BoNeZu

A studio built on craft, curiosity, and weird ideas.

Founded in 2021, BoNeZu started as a side project between three friends who couldn't stop prototyping. Today we're a tight-knit independent studio working on original IP across multiple platforms.

We believe games are at their best when they take risks. Every project we ship begins with a question we don't already know the answer to.
